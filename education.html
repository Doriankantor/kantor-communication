Education

Education

Learning a language is not about collecting grammar rules. It is about being able to say what you mean, understand what you hear, and feel confident doing both. Our method is communicative from the first lesson: we build the vocabulary you actually need, develop fluency through real use, and grow the confidence that turns knowledge into speech.

Founder video — placeholder · Dorian Kantor welcomes learners

Every student learns from materials chosen for their specific level — not a one-size-fits-all syllabus. And every instructor is quality-controlled and trained in our teaching methodology, so the experience is consistent whoever you study with.

This track is for members of the community — learners of any age and any profession.

We teach more than language skills. We build intercultural competence — the cultural understanding that turns a correct sentence into a real connection — because knowing how to say something is only half of being understood.

How lessons work

  • Communicative method — focused on building vocabulary, developing fluency, and growing confidence in real use.
  • Materials matched to your level — selected for you, not pulled from a generic course.
  • Trained instructors — quality-controlled and trained in our methodology.
  • 50-minute lessons.
  • Homework on request — assigned to match how much you want to do between lessons.
  • Flexible packages — from one to five lessons per week.
