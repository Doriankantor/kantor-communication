FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers for both tracks — choose Consulting or Language School above.

What does it cost? ›

Because every engagement is scoped to your goals, audience, and format, we propose pricing as a tailored package rather than a fixed list. We recommend the right package after a short discovery call, so the work stays focused on what you actually need.

What does it cost? ›

Because every course is scoped to your goals, level, and format, we propose pricing as a tailored package rather than a fixed list. We suggest the right package in your free first consultation, so the work stays focused on what you actually need.

How do we get started? ›

With a free, no-obligation first conversation — about 15–20 minutes — to understand your level, your goals, and your timeline. From there we propose how we'd work together.

Which languages do you offer? ›

English, German, Hungarian, Spanish, and Italian.

What levels do you work with? ›

Our consulting work is designed for professionals, executives, institutions, and researchers who already work in the language and want to communicate in it with more precision and authority.

What levels do you work with? ›

All levels, from beginner to advanced. We find the right starting point together in the first consultation.

How long is a session? ›

Consulting sessions are scoped to the engagement.

How long is a lesson? ›

Lessons are 50 minutes.

How flexible is scheduling? ›

Very. We work around your calendar — early mornings, evenings, or weekends — and arrange times directly with your trainer.

Is it online or in person? ›

Primarily online, which lets us work with you wherever you are; specific arrangements are discussed in the first conversation.

Is everything confidential? ›

Yes. Consulting engagements are confidential throughout.
