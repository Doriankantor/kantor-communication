FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers for both tracks. Tags mark whether an answer applies to Consulting, Education, or both.

What does it cost? Both›

Because every engagement is scoped to your goals, level, and format, we propose pricing as a tailored package rather than a fixed list. Consulting For consulting, we recommend the right package after a short discovery call. Education For lessons, we suggest the right package in your free first consultation. This keeps the work focused on what you actually need.

How do we get started? Both›

With a free, no-obligation first conversation — about 15–20 minutes — to understand your level, your goals, and your timeline. From there we propose how we'd work together.

Which languages do you offer? Both›

English, German, Hungarian, Spanish, and Italian.

What levels do you work with? Education · Consulting›

Education All levels, from beginner to advanced. We find the right starting point together in the first consultation. Consulting Our consulting work is designed for advanced professionals, executives, institutions, and researchers.

How long is a session? Both›

Education lessons are 50 minutes. Consulting sessions are scoped to the engagement.

How flexible is scheduling? Both›

Very. We work around your calendar — early mornings, evenings, or weekends — and arrange times directly with your trainer.

Is it online or in person? Both›

Primarily online, which lets us work with you wherever you are; specific arrangements are discussed in the first conversation.

Is everything confidential? Consulting›

Yes. Consulting engagements are confidential throughout.
